
# High Hanging Fruit — Website Plan

## Brand & Design System
- **Color palette**: Earthy greens, warm terracotta/amber, deep teal accent, clean white backgrounds
- **Typography**: Inter font, confident and approachable tone
- **Custom logo**: Text-based "High Hanging Fruit" with a subtle leaf/soil icon element
- **Overall feel**: Mediterranean warmth, modern precision agriculture

---

## Pages

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero section** with bold headline ("Smarter Soil. Resilient Crops. Every Drop Counts."), subheadline about helping Málaga mango farmers, parallax-style background image of mango groves
- Two CTAs: "Request Your Soil Kit" (primary) and "Explore the Platform" (ghost button)
- **The Problem** section with 3 icon-stat cards (water stress, shifting climate windows, soil variability)
- **Three-Pillar Approach** section with expandable/interactive cards:
  - Soil & Water Intelligence
  - Climate-Resilient Planning
  - Water Management & Advisory
- **Navigation bar** with links to all pages, sticky header

### 2. Platform Preview Page (`/platform`)
- Interactive mockup of the farmer dashboard using tabs:
  - **Regional Trends View**: Map-style visualization of Málaga showing soil health, rainfall, temperature trends (mock data)
  - **My Farm View**: Personalized dashboard with soil results, climate outlook, water recommendations, alerts (mock data)
  - **"What If" Scenario Tool**: Interactive demo where users can toggle variables (variety, planting month) and see projected outcomes (mock data)
- "Coming Soon" badge for Sensor Integration feature
- CTA to request a soil kit

### 3. Request Soil Kit Page (`/request-kit`)
- Clean form with fields: name, farm name, location, farm size, contact info, message
- UI only (no submission logic), with a success state preview
- Brief explanation of what the soil kit includes and the process

### 4. About Page (`/about`)
- Mission statement and company story
- Why Málaga mangos, why now
- Team section (placeholder)
- "Spanish version coming soon" note

### 5. Contact Page (`/contact`)
- Contact form (UI only)
- Location info (Málaga, Spain)
- Social media links (placeholder)

---

## Shared Components
- **Sticky navigation** with logo and page links
- **Footer** with quick links, contact info, and "Spanish version coming soon" note
- **Responsive design** — mobile-friendly throughout
- Smooth scroll animations and hover interactions for a polished feel
